Did this come on suddenly?

⚠ When a change comes on fast, it's worth a quick medical check

Sudden behavior changes in dementia are often caused by something physical and treatable:

  • UTI
  • Pain
  • Constipation
  • Dehydration
  • Infection / fever
  • Medication change

Comfort and a medical check aren't either/or. If this behavior is new, consider contacting their healthcare provider.
